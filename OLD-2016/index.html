ABOUT ME

Aspiring Engineer, and everything in between

Welcome, my name is John Daniel Atanasov and this is my personal website. A collection of certifications, accomplishments, projects I have worked on and of course my résumé are all here, updated in real-time. I am a twelfth grade senior at Saint François Xavier from Sarnia Ontario, fluent in three languages and hold a strong passion for mechanical, electrical and software engineering.

Currently conditionally accepted to the University of Waterloo Mechatronics Engineering program, I strive to expand my technical knowledge and apply it, with my leadership capabilities, to multiple engineering projects. Along the way my drive for learning has also had me doing video editing, graphic design and even scientific research projects.
