About Me

"The optimist thinks this is the best of all possible worlds. The pessimist fears it is true."- R. Oppenheimer

Welcome, my name is John and this is my personal website. A collection of certifications, accomplishments, projects I have worked on and of course my résumé are all here, updated as much as possible. I am currently a Mechatronics Engineering undergrad student at the University of Waterloo, graduate of Saint François Xavier French Highschool from Sarnia Ontario, fluent in three languages and I hold a strong passion for mechanical, electrical and software engineering.

I strive to expand my technical knowledge and apply it when I can. From my leadership capabilities to my strong work ethic, I have worked on multiple team and personal engineering focused projects. Along the way, my drive for learning has also had me doing video editing, graphic design and even scientific research projects.

Oppenheimer has been a truely inspirational figure for me ever since I did a reseach project on him back in Highschool. Whenever possible I bring up his quote above because I find myself asking: "Where does the realist stand?". My answer is that a realist understands the philosophical situation, but turns to action instead of being consumed by emotions. That is why I try to solve any problem I can see.

My life is not just about my work or education, even if I find everything that I do just as interesting. Most of my projects, such as video edits and image manipulation all came from me trying to develop my hobbies. Among the other hobbies I hold, hiking, playing sports such as basketball and badminton, and of course playing video games are all things that define me.

After having had the opportunity of traveling far and wide, I can honestly say that my experiences are one of the driving factors pushing me to pursue my academic and career goals. Everyone, everywhere has a problem that needs to be addressed, and I can only hope that I have what it takes to make the world a slightly better place.
